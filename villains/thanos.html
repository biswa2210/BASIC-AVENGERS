THANOS  BOSS OF UNIVERSE

ABOUT  THANOS

Thanos is a mutant member of the race of superhumans known as the Titanian Eternals. The character possesses abilities common to the Eternals, but amplified to a higher degree through a combination of his mutant–Eternalheritage, amplification and power bestowed by the abstract entity, Death.Thanos's first appearance was in The Invincible Iron Man #55 (February 1973), featuring a story by Jim Starlin that was co-scripted by Mike Friedrich. The storyline from that issue continued through Captain Marvel (bi-monthly: March 1973 – Jan. 1974), Marvel Feature(Nov. 1973), Daredevil (Jan. 1974), and Avengers (July 1974). He returned in an extended storyline that spanned Strange Tales (Feb.–Aug. 1975), Warlock (Oct. 1975 – Jan.1976), Marvel Team Up(March 1977), and the 1977 Annuals for Avengers and Marvel Two-in-One (Thanos does not actually appear until the end of Warlock ). He was also featured in a short backup story in Logan's Run (June 1977)and had a small role in the Death of Captain Marvel graphic novel (April 1982).
