BLACK DWARF

 Cull Obsidian was physically the most powerful of Thanos' children and was a member of the Black Order. He joined his adoptive father Thanos in his quest to obtain all the Infinity Stones. He and his siblings massacred half of the Asgardians, attacked Greenwich Village, and fought in the Battle of Wakanda, where he was killed by Bruce Banner in the Hulkbuster suit.
