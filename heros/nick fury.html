Samuel L. Jackson plays the character in MCU, Nicholas Joseph "Nick" Fury is a world-renowned spy, former Director of S.H.I.E.L.D. and founder of the Avengers. Fury began his career within the United States Army in the late 1960s, rising to the rank of Colonel before his honorable discharge. He later became a CIA operative during the Cold War, primarily operating in Soviet territory. Continuing to serve the world, Fury joined S.H.I.E.L.D. to fight against Earth's most dangerous enemies.Fury refocused his efforts over the next 15 years to organize the Avengers Initiative, with Fury's true intentions were to bring together any other powerful individuals to assist S.H.I.E.L.D. with protecting the Earth from extraterrestrial threats. He appears in IRONMAN(2008), IRONMAN 2(2010), THE AVENGERS(2012), CAPTAIN AMERICA: THE WINTER SOLDIER(2014), AVENGERS: AGE OF ULTRON(2015), CAPTAIN MARVEL(2019), AVENGERS: ENDGAME(2019).
