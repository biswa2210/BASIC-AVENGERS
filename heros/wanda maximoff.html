Elizabeth Olsen plays the character of Wanda Maximoff, who is a native of Sokovia who grew up with her fraternal twin brother, Pietro. In an effort to help purge their country of strife, the twins joined HYDRA and agreed to undergo experiments with the Scepter under the supervision of Wolfgang von Strucker. While her brother developed super-speed, Wanda attained various abilities including telekinesis, telepathy, and reality manipulation. When HYDRA fell, the twins joined Ultron to get their revenge on Tony Stark over his weapons killing their family in a bombing, but eventually abandoned him when they discovered Ultron's true intentions to destroy Earth. Wanda and Pietro fought with the Avengers during the Battle of Sokovia to stop Ultron; Pietro was killed during the battle but Wanda survived, and shortly after Ultron's destruction, became a member of the Avengers.
