Benedict Cumberbatch playes the character, Stephen Vincent Strange M.D., Ph.D is a powerful sorcerer and Master of the Mystic Arts. Originally a brilliant, although arrogant, neurosurgeon, Strange got into a car accident which resulted with his hands becoming crippled. When all Western medicine failed him, Strange embarked on a journey that led him into Kamar-Taj where Strange had made the discovery of magic and alternate dimensions, being trained by the Ancient One. He got the responsibility to protect the TIME STONE. He appears in DOCTOR STRANGE(2016), AVENGERS:INFINITY WAR(2018), AVENGERS:ENDGAME(2019).
