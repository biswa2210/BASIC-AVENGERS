GUARDIANS OF THE GALAXY

The Guardians of the Galaxy are a band of intergalactic outlaws, who teamed together to protect the galaxy from planetary threats. In 2014, they managed to save the planet of Xandar from Ronan the Accuser and have been recognized under the Nova Empire. Several months later, they defeated the Abilisk, attracted the unwelcome attentions of Ayesha and her Sovereign fleet, and defeated Ego. Four years after that, the Guardians joined forces with the Avengers to stop Thanos from collecting the Infinity Stones.
