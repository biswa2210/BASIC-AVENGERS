Dave Bautista plays the character, Drax is a former Kylosian intergalactic criminal, and a member of the Guardians of the Galaxy. He sought revenge on Ronan the Accuser for killing his wife and daughter, and went on a rampage across the galaxy, ending with him being imprisoned by the Nova Corps in the Kyln. There, Drax became uneasy allies with Star-Lord, Gamora, Rocket Raccoon, and Groot.
Four years later, the Guardians of the Galaxy joined up with Thor and the Avengers in trying to stop Thanos from obtaining the Infinity Stones.
He appears in Guardians of The Galaxy vol1 (2014), Guardians of The Galaxy vol2 (2017), AVENGERS:INFINITY WAR (2018), AVENGERS:ENDGAME(2019).
